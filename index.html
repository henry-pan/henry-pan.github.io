Resume

I am a Software Engineer with a passion for building products. During my spare time, I am an independent game developer, creator of Studio Xehryn. My interests mostly pertain to game development, like game design, creative writing, worldbuilding, music, and digital art. Outside of that, I also enjoy weightlifting, anime, and cosmology.

I've been writing code since high school and have long been interested in computer technology. This led me to study at University of California, Santa Cruz to further my knowledge, earning a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. There I learned a variety of topics and skills, including Android development, web development, algorithms, databases, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. Afterwards, I discovered full stack development and decided to attend App Academy, gaining expertise in frameworks like React and Rails.

With my experience from both university and App Academy, I am now seeking a full-time software engineering opportunity to further develop my skills technically and professionally.

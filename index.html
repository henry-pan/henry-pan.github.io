About Me

I am a Software Engineer with a passion for building products, and experienced in JavaScript, Ruby, React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, HTML5, and CSS3.

During my spare time, I work as an independent game developer. I enjoy game design, creative writing, worldbuilding, music, and digital art as well. As a result, I created an independent game studio (Studio Xehryn) with my fellow artists to develop quality games for players to experience and enjoy.

I've been writing code since high school and have been interested in computer technology ever since childhood. This led me to study at University of California, Santa Cruz to further my knowledge, earning a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. There I learned a variety of topics and skills, including Android development, web development, algorithms, databases, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. Afterwards, I discovered fullstack development and decided to attend App Academy.

With my experience from both university and App Academy, I am now seeking a full-time software engineering opportunity to further develop my skills technically and professionally.

This Android app gathers the airing times/dates of Japanese animated TV shows (known as Anime) and provides a method of keeping track of them via the Android Calendar.
